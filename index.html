Creators

GTA 5 was created by the company Rockstar North, its distributor is Rockstar games, the designer Leslie Benzies Imran Sarwar and the director Steve Martin.

Theme

It is an open world action adventure with a multitude of missions in which we can play with three different characters: Trevor, Michael and Franklin. In addition, it has GTA Online, the online multiplayer mode of GTA 5 with many missions in which you create the character and you can play them, the character has its own level and money within the game, you can play in sessions of up to 30 players For each one, you can also only play with friends on your same platform since the game does not have crossplay (the ability to play with a different console than yours at the same time).

Top seller

GTA V is the second best-selling game in the world with a figure of 160 million copies sold, which represents a total profit, not counting GTA online, of 6,000 million dollars since its premiere in 2013.

GTA VI

GTA 6 is a long-awaited game by the gta community since we have gta 5 since 2013 and until today we do not have any version of gta 6. Rockstar Games announced a few months ago that they were developing this new game, which only It will reach the consoles of the new generation which are the PS5 and the Xbox series X and S. It is expected that this game will be the bomb of the bombs with very high and good graphics, better gameplay, the map much larger than the one we have now. It is estimated that this game will be released between 2024 and 2025.

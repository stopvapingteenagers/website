What is a Vape?

A vape is an electronic device that heats a liquid, turning it into vapor that you inhale. Most vapes contain nicotine, a highly addictive substance that affects the brain and body negatively.

Fun Facts

  • Many vape liquids contain more nicotine than a pack of cigarettes.
  • An electronic cigarrette contains heavy metals and toxic chemicals.
  • Teens who vape are more likely to start smoking cigarettes later and die at a much younger age.
  • Nicotine can harm brain development and functionality in teens.

How to Stop Vaping

If you want to quit vaping, here are some steps that help:

  • Tell a friend or adult you trust.
  • Replace vaping with a healthy habit.
  • Use nicotine-free alternatives if needed.
  • Look for professional help if it becomes difficult.
